What is Velocity Tennis Academy?

Welcome to Velocity Tennis Academy, where speed meets skill and passion ignites potential! At Velocity Tennis Academy, we are dedicated to providing top-notch tennis instruction to aspiring players of all ages and abilities. Our experienced coaches are committed to helping every student reach their full potential on the court. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or striving to take your game to the next level, our comprehensive program is designed to meet your needs and goals.

At Velocity Tennis Academy, we focus on developing fundamental skills, including proper grip, footwork, stroke mechanics, and strategy. Through dynamic drills, personalized instruction, and match play, students will build confidence, improve technique, and enhance their overall game. Our supportive and inclusive environment fosters sportsmanship among our students, creating lifelong memories and connections both on and off the court.

Join us at Velocity Tennis Academy and experience the thrill of tennis in motion. With dedication, determination, and a dash of velocity, the sky's the limit for what you can achieve in the world of tennis. Let's hit the courts and unleash your full potential together!

Classes

Beginner

Dive into the exciting world of tennis with our Beginner Course! Perfect for those new to the sport, this course focuses on mastering the fundamentals including:

  • Proper grip
  • Balanced stance
  • Basic strokes
  • Appropriate positioning
  • Developed footwork
Our experienced coaches will guide you through each step, building confidence and laying the groundwork for your tennis journey.

Intermediate

Ready to take your game to the next level? Our Intermediate Course is designed for players looking to refine their skills and elevate their performance on the court. We take your pre-existing skills and improve them in greater ways:

  • Greater consistency
  • Precise aim
  • Improved footwork
  • General conditioning
  • Steady rallies
This level of coaching will allow students to develop a deeper understanding of the game.

Advanced

Take your tennis game to new heights with our Advanced Course! Designed for experienced players seeking to fine-tune their abilities and compete at a higher level, this course offers intensive training in advanced techniques such as:

  • Tactical strategies
  • Expert gameplay
  • Advanced conditioning
  • Finishing the point
  • Skillful shots
Our expert coaches will challenge you to push your limits, unlock your full potential, and achieve success on the court.

Meet the Founders

Welcome to Velocity Tennis Academy, founded by a family passionate about tennis and dedicated to helping students unlock their full potential on the court.

Pranav Vaddepalli, a seasoned tennis player with a string of tournament victories under his belt, knows firsthand the thrill of competition and the joy of mastering the game. As the varsity 1st singles player at Lawrence High School, Pranav brings his expertise and love for tennis to Velocity Tennis Academy, where he aims to inspire the next generation of players.

Joining him in this endeavor is his sister, Laasyasri Vaddepalli, who shares Pranav's passion for tennis and has also made her mark on the court with numerous tournament wins. As the former varsity 2nd singles player during her high school years, Laasyasri brings her competitive spirit and dedication to excellence to Velocity Tennis Academy.

Completing the founding trio is their father, Srinath Vaddepalli, whose expert coaching and unwavering support have been instrumental in shaping Pranav and Laasyasri into the accomplished players they are today. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the game, Srinath brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to Velocity Tennis Academy, ensuring that every student receives top-notch training and guidance.
